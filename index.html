About me

I am a first-year PhD student at Mila – Québec AI Institute in Montréal, where I am fortunate to be advised by Amir-massoud Farahmand. I’m broadly interested in reinforcement learning and the theoretical foundations of deep learning, aiming to bridge mathematical understanding with methods that work in the real world.

Previously, I completed an MSc and a BSc at Tel Aviv University, under the supervision of Nadav Cohen and Mahmood Sharif.
