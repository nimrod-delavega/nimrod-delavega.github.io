About me

I am a PhD student in Computer Engineering (Reinforcement Learning) at Mila / Polytechnique Montréal, supervised by Prof. Amir-Massoud Farahmand.

My research centers on acceleration, stability, and data efficiency in reinforcement learning and theoretical deep learning. I’m especially interested in optimization-theoretic views of RL and continual adaptation.
